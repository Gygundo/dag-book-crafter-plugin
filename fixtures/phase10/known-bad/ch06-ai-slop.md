# Chapter 6: The Believer and His Words

Your words carry power. It is important to note that the tongue is not just a small member of the body, it is the rudder that steers your whole life. Prayer is more than just words you recite. In today's fast-paced world, many Christians delve into every subject except the one that matters most.

> *Death and life are in the power of THE TONGUE: and they that love it shall eat the fruit thereof.*
> -- Proverbs 18:21

In other words, your mouth is planting seeds every single day. You will eat the harvest of the words you sow. Watch what you say about your family. Watch what you say about your future.

> *But I say unto you, That every idle word that men shall speak, THEY SHALL GIVE ACCOUNT thereof in the day of judgment.*
> -- Matthew 12:36

Notice that Jesus did not say some words. He said every idle word. You must take your words seriously because Heaven takes them seriously.

> *Set a watch, O LORD, before my mouth; KEEP THE DOOR OF MY LIPS.*
> -- Psalm 141:3

Make this your prayer today. Guard your mouth and you will guard your life!
