# Chapter 4: No Version Stamp

Sarah carried the mug back inside at 7:14am and set it on the counter. The kitchen was quiet in the way only an old house can be quiet — full of small sounds that almost count as silence.

The Greek word *charis* sat in her notebook, underlined twice.

> *"What if grace is the only thing I actually need to remember today?"*

She picked up her pen and wrote one sentence. Then she wrote another. The page filled itself slowly, the way all honest pages do.

> *"What if writing it down is part of believing it?"*

The clock on the wall ticked steadily. The light kept moving. Sarah kept writing.
