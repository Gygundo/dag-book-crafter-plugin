Sarah walked outside at 7:02am, mug still in hand. The grass was wet and the air smelled like rain that had not yet arrived. She was thinking about what her mother used to say.

The word for grace she had learned was *charis*. Just the one. It was enough.

> *"What if today is the day I stop performing?"*

She crossed the small garden, sat on the wooden bench her father had built, and watched the clouds shift over the rooftops.

> *"What if rest is the actual work?"*

The neighbour's dog barked once, then went quiet. Sarah closed her eyes and listened to nothing in particular.
