# Chapter 5: Silent Reader

Sarah sat down at the kitchen table at 7:30am with her notebook open in front of her. The sun had risen properly now and the room was warm enough to feel friendly.

The Greek word *charis* still held her attention. She underlined it a third time, just to be sure.

She wrote a single line about her mother. Then a line about her father. Then a line about herself, which was the hardest of the three.

The clock on the wall ticked steadily. A car passed outside. Sarah kept writing until the page was full and then turned to the next one.

The morning kept moving. Sarah kept moving with it. There was no hurry now and there had not been for some time.
